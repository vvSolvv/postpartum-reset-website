Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about The Postpartum Reset

When should I start the Postpartum Reset?

You can begin as soon as you're cleared by your doctor for normal activity (usually 6 weeks postpartum). However, the guide is flexible and beneficial at any stage in the first year after birth.

How much time does it require each day?

The daily practices are designed to take just 5-15 minutes. We believe consistency with small actions creates better results than occasional big efforts that are hard to maintain.

Is this safe while breastfeeding?

Yes! All recommendations are breastfeeding-friendly. We avoid extreme diet changes and focus on nourishing foods and gentle practices that support milk production.

What if I don't see results right away?

Hormone balancing is a gradual process. Most moms notice increased energy within 2-3 weeks, with more significant changes appearing by week 6-8. We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee.
